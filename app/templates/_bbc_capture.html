Ringing Room: A Virtual Change-Ringing Platform

Welcome to Ringing Room! The video above will give you an introduction to English change-ringing and to Ringing Room, a virtual platform used by change-ringers all over the world.

Think you'd like to give it a go? Click here to join a tower specifically for BBC watchers. Or, if you'd like a more guided experience, we have friendly, helpful ringers running guided practices at regular intervals through the week. The times and dates are posted below.

If you have any troubles, please feel free to reach out on Facebook or by email. Looking forward to seeing you up the tower!
