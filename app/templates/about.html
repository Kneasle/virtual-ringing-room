The site

Welcome to the Ringing Room! This is a site built for change ringers to continue ringing with one another even when socially distanced. It is under development currently, and new features may show up with little warning. Please contact us if you have any issues or suggestions.

The developers

The Ringing Room is a joint venture by Leland Paul Kusmer and Bryn Marie Reinstadler, both ringers in the Boston, Massachusetts area.

The audio

The tower bell audio is provided by Bill Hibbert. The handbell recordings are of Leland's bells, with editing and extension by JES.
