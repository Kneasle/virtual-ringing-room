We decided to create the Ringing Room in order to facilitate continued engagement with ringing during the coronavirus crisis.

During this time of crisis, submissions to BellBoard have slowed to a crawl. We encourage all who ring on this platform and submit to BellBoard to donate to The Ringing World.

There are also ongoing costs associated with sustaining the Ringing Room; if you'd like to help us keep the site up and running, we're accepting donations by Venmo or Paypal. Thank you!

Donations are first allocated to ensuring we have a 12-month rolling supply of funding for server and tool costs. Further donations go to support the on-going development costs of Ringing Room in terms of labor and time.
